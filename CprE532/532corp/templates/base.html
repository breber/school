Password Audit

As a security consulting company, we need to be sure to keep our own security standards up to date. In order to help do this, our IT department has decided to perform an audit of all the passwords used in our system. To facilitate this, we ask that you enter all passwords used on our domain into this form. On submission of this form, you will be warned if your password has been deemed insecure.
