Electronic Baseline

The Making of the Video

I didn't think this assignment would be that difficult, because I greatly enjoy using technology. I found it to be somewhat difficult actually. First off, I didn't have a decent video camera. I tried taking video on my cell phone, but that video was of poor quality. I then decided to take still pictures and stitch them together like a slideshow, and add some background music. This was the extent of my difficulties with the technology aspect.

I had a pretty good idea of what I wanted to film/depict. I knew of some great places around campus, and I had some pretty good pictures of these places. My next problem was that I had too many pictures to fit in a 30 second time slot, so I had to go through and choose the best pictures for the final cut. So the content wasn't the hard part, it was just trying to put it together in a good way.
