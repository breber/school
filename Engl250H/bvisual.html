Visual Baseline

I would have to say that this was one of the easier tasks. I had a few places in mind when I was assigned to go take a picture on campus. First was the Campanile. I figured that this space is pretty well known, so if I were to do it, I would need to find a unique way to photograph it. I was thinking about getting up early, or going out in the evening at sunset to get a cool picture with the sun and the sky.

A few days before the visual baseline was due I found a cool "statue-like" object that I thought was pretty cool. It is right outside Coover Hall, which probably isn't visited by that many people. It is mainly a computer and electrical engineering building. It is also on the edge of campus, so most people don't normally walk by it. This statue is in remembrance of the first computer bug. I am not sure if many people know the story behind the word "bug" as it relates to computers. The first computer bug occurred a long time ago, and it was actually a bug in the computer that caused it to fail. I thought that the story behind this statue, and the statue itself interested me, which is why I ended up using this location for my visual baseline.
