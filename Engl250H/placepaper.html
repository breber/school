Place Paper

One of our first assignments was to write a paper about a place. I decided to write my paper about the Parks Library. To do this paper, we needed to visit and document the place we chose to research. I went to the library one afternoon, and collected a bunch of pictures that I thought did a good job at portraying the unique atmosphere that the library provides.
