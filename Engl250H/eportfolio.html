About the ePortfolio

Welcome to my final project for English 250H, Section PB. Throughout this semester we learned about communication through different modes. This has been an interesting and insightful experience. The purpose of this site is to share all of my work from throughout the semester in an electronic portfolio.

In reality, all of the projects projects are multi-modal in some sense because it is difficult to use only one mode. For example, our papers can be classified as either written or visual; they have components of both in them. So in this site, I will be classifying each project by its primary mode.

On the left side of the page, there is a naviagtion bar. This bar is built so that when you hover over a certain section, it will expand giving you a list of the different activities that fall into that category. I chose this technique to save space and clutter in the menubar area.

This site is made to work in the most recent version of Firefox. Some of the "eye-candy" features, such as some of the hover features, will only work in Firefox. I am able to get them to work in other browsers, but for the sake of this project, I only need it to work well in Firefox.

Feel free to visit any and all parts of the site. Enjoy!

-Brian Reber
